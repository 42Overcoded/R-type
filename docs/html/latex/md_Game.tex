R-\/\+Type is a classic side-\/scrolling shoot \textquotesingle{}em up (shmup) video game that was first released in the arcades by Irem in 1987. ~\newline
 It has since become an iconic and influential title in the shoot \textquotesingle{}em up genre. ~\newline
 The game is known for its challenging gameplay, unique power-\/up system, and distinctive visual style.

\hypertarget{md_Game_autotoc_md28}{}\doxysubsection{Side Scrolling Gameplay}\label{md_Game_autotoc_md28}
R-\/\+Type is played on a 2D side-\/scrolling plane. ~\newline
 Your spaceship, known as the R-\/9, moves from left to right through a series of horizontally scrolling levels, facing a variety of enemy spacecraft, obstacles, and boss battles.

~\newline
\hypertarget{md_Game_autotoc_md29}{}\doxysubsection{Objective}\label{md_Game_autotoc_md29}
The main objective of R-\/\+Type is to progress through the game\textquotesingle{}s levels, defeating waves of enemies and ultimately facing and defeating the powerful end-\/of-\/level bosses. ~\newline
 Your goal is to complete all the levels and save the universe from the Bydo, an alien race that threatens Earth.

\hypertarget{md_Game_autotoc_md30}{}\doxysubsection{Life system}\label{md_Game_autotoc_md30}
In R-\/\+Type, you have a limited number of lives, and when you lose all of them, you\textquotesingle{}ll need to restart at the beginning of the level.
